# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

EMPIRE ZERO respects the privacy of its users.

The bot only processes the information required to provide its Discord features. No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

Information that may be processed includes:
- Discord User IDs
- Server IDs
- Channel IDs
- Messages used with bot commands

This information is used only for the operation of the bot.

If you have any questions, contact the bot developer through Discord.
